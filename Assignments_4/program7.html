7) Maze Escape  
Problem:  
You are given a 2D grid (matrix) representing a maze where 0 = free path and 1 = wall. Start 
from (0,0) and try moving right or down only. Stop all loops immediately once you reach the 
bottom-right corner. Print "Escaped" or "Blocked".  Use nested loops with labeled break. 
Sample Test Cases:  
[[0,0,1],[1,0,0],[1,0,0]] → “Escaped” (path along zeros)  
[[0,1],[1,0]] → “Blocked”
