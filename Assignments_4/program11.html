11) Nested Ternary Classification (8 Categories) 
Problem:  
Given a number n, classify it as: 
"Small Positive Even" 
"Small Positive Odd" 
"Large Positive Even" 
"Large Positive Odd" 
"Small Negative Even" 
"Small Negative Odd" 
"Large Negative Even" 
"Large Negative Odd" 
Where "Small" = |n| < 100 and "Large" = |n| >= 100. Use only nested ternary operators. 
Sample Test Cases:  
n=42 → “Small Positive Even” - n=-101 → “Large Negative Odd”
